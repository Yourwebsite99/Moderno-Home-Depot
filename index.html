Questions

Before you reach out.

Do you handle full renovations or only supply materials?

Both. You can order materials directly, or bring us in from concept through installation with our in-house design and fitting team.

How long does a kitchen or bathroom project take?

A bathroom typically runs 4–6 weeks from confirmed design; a full kitchen is 8–12 weeks, largely dictated by stone and cabinetry lead times.

Can I see materials in person before ordering?

Yes — our showroom holds full slabs, tile panels and working fixtures, and we encourage a visit before any final decision.

Do you offer warranties on installed work?

All installations carry a 10-year workmanship warranty; material warranties vary by manufacturer and are provided at order.

Do you ship materials outside the region?

We ship nationally for material-only orders. Full design and installation service is currently limited to our local service area.
